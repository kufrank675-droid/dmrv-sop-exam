# Product

## Register

product

## Users

DMRV project operators, maintenance supervisors, regional maintenance technicians, and platform administrators who need to practice, assess, and audit Maintenance Management SOP knowledge in Chinese or English.

## Product Purpose

The product turns the Maintenance Management SOP question bank into a bilingual online assessment surface. Success means users can start practice or exam sessions quickly, review SOP-grounded explanations, manage imported question-bank data, and retain score records suitable for later DMRV platform integration.

## Brand Personality

Precise, operational, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a dependable field-operations console rather than a marketing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative landing-page composition, oversized hero statistics, generic SaaS gradients, playful quiz-game styling, low-density card grids, and ambiguous controls. The product should not look like a public course website or a consumer trivia app.

## Design Principles

1. Put the task first: the first screen should expose assessment state, question-bank health, and immediate entry points.
2. Preserve audit confidence: every question should retain answer, explanation, SOP location, and source file.
3. Keep bilingual switching predictable: Chinese and English should share layout and controls.
4. Favor dense but calm layouts: show enough operational information without decoration that competes with the task.
5. Make state visible: selected answers, active mode, completion progress, and results must be legible at a glance.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus, 44px minimum interactive targets, no horizontal scroll on mobile, reduced-motion support, and non-color-only status cues.
